Diori App Design

Social Traveling App for Android and Iphone

Diori App

Diori is an aplication for searching toursim information around Indonesia. The information is provided by the goverment and by croudsourcing.

The information that served by the aplication contain the 4A information, those are Attraction, Accomodation, Accesibilites, and Amenities.
The information about Hotels, route, facilites, travel destination, and also Travel blog

Diori has feature to post feed and blog travel so that the user can contribute to the application.

The development of this application is on progress , and my role in this development is as a Front-end
The application has a PWA version a sample version, access at https://diori.herokuapp.com/

my role (in team):

  • Create and mantain the design

  • Develop the app design with React.js
